CSE

CSE stands for Computer Science and Engineering, an academic field that focuses on the theory and design of computational systems, including both software and hardware. It involves the study of information, computation, algorithms, programming languages, and the development of software and computer hardware. Graduates with a CSE degree acquire skills in areas like artificial intelligence, cybersecurity, cloud computing, and data structures, opening doors to diverse career paths in tech and beyond.
